Q3. What is the role of a rera approved real estate agent?

Ans. A real estate agent has to undergo special training and has to clear the examination prescribed by MAHARERA. He then gets the Approval No. to act as an agent for offering services to the clients. No person other than the approved RERA Agent can be involved in transactions related to any property.

Q6. What costs are involved in buying a property?

Ans. Purchase price of the property, Stamp Duty and Registration Fees at the time of registration and Processing Fees and legal fees payable to Bank if loan required.

Q7. What factors should I consider before buying property?

Ans. Before buying property, consider factors such as location, property condition, market trends, financing options, potential rental income (if applicable), and your long-term investment goals. Consulting with a real estate professional from “Niwaskhoj” can also provide valuable guidance.

Q8. What are the different types of rental properties “Niwaskhoj” deals in?

Q9. What are common amenities and special features one should look for in a property?

Ans. Common amenities in properties may include the location of the property, Security aspect, proximity from important places, parking spaces, recreational facilities like children park, swiming pool, Gym etc.

Q10. What is a resale or buying and selling of existing property ?

Ans. A resale property is a real estate asset which is already owned by the person and he wants to sell it to another person for any reason. RERA Act is not applicable in such case. It is very important to verify the title of such property and encumbrances on it before buying that property.
